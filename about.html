Biryani is a layered rice dish that's popular in South Asia and is often made with meat, vegetables, and spices. The word "biryani" comes from the Farsi phrase "birinj biriyan," which means "fried rice". Here are some things you can include in biryani restaurant content: Spices Biryani is often made with a variety of spices, including star anise, cloves, peppercorn, cardamom, cinnamon, cumin, red chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, coriander powder, turmeric, and salt.
